The Heritage

PHASE 01: THE SEED

We scoured the high-altitude Andes for the rarest, starch-perfect heirloom potatoes. No generics. Only pure, unadulterated spud DNA.

PHASE 02: THE DIRT

Planted in mineral-rich volcanic soil and watered by glacial runoff. Our farming partners employ brutalist agriculture—tough love makes for a crispier bite.

PHASE 03: THE CUT

Precision geometry. Our potatoes are laser-measured and cut into exact structural shapes to maximize surface area for ultimate crunch.

PHASE 04: THE FRY

Double-fried in pure Wagyu tallow at exactly 375°F. Flash-dusted with high-voltage spice blends while still screaming hot.
